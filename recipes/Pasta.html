Pasta Salad

This chili relleno casserole is very easy to prepare and is loaded with flavor. It's great for a busy weeknight and good enough for company.

    Ingredients:
  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables
    Steps:
  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
